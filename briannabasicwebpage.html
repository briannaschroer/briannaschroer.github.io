About me

"I have no idea what I am doing"

I'm from St. Louis, Missouri and will graduate from the University of Missouri in December. I knew designing was in my future as soon as I started bonding with my co-workers over a mutual hatred of comic sans, love of white space and caffeine dependency. When I'm not geeking out over fonts and color palettes, I enjoy working out, binge-watching The Office and eating cool food.

I am currently designing at The Virginian-Pilot.
