BRIANNA'S DESIGN DOCUMENT

Purpose:

This website will act as my personal portfolio. There are two main purposes for this website. First, I want this to be an easy way for potenial employers to view my latest work. To ensure this, I will need to update my website regularly. Second, I want my website to be able to give employers an easy way to contact me.



Audience:

The target audience for this website will be newspaper designers and editors. This group of people probably goes through a ton of portfolios a day, so I need to give them an easy and fast way to view my work.



Needs Assessment:

Four potential employeers gave me insight on what they would want to see on a designer's website. Employeers want to see a nice, clean website that doesn't distract from a designer's work. They also belive that quality is a lot more important than quanity when they are looking at your deisgn clips.

Questions asked to the group:

  • What type of information would you find helpful on a potential applicant's website?
  • An easy way to contact an applicant
  • Their best work
  • An updated resume
  • A little bit about the designer, so we know if they would fit in with the company

  • What is most important to you when you are viewing someone's portfolio?
  • A clean wesbite that doesn't distract from the designer's work
  • Only their best work
  • Good flow

  • What kinds of content would you not like to see on an applicants portfolio?
  • Highschool work
  • Spelling errors
  • An overload of clips


Content:

Based on the responses from the potential employers, I will include the following sections:

ABOUT PAGE:

CONTACT:

  • This will give potential employers a place where they can find my email, social media, phone number, ect.

WORK:

  • This page will be orgazined by publications that I work at. It will have a summary of what I do at the publication and photos of my best clips.

HOME PAGE

  • This will give a brief intoduction to what you will find on the website.


Presentation of Information/Rationale of Organization:

This website will be organized from newest to oldest work
